Cardio

At our lab, we offer advanced cardiac treatments, including cardiac catheterization and percutaneous coronary interventions. These minimally invasive procedures diagnose and treat conditions like coronary artery disease by opening blocked arteries to improve blood flow. Utilizing state-of-the-art technology, our skilled team ensures optimal heart health and provides tailored follow-up care for a smooth recovery.

Neurology

At our lab, we provide cutting-edge neurological treatments, including advanced imaging and minimally invasive procedures for diagnosing and managing conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative disorders. Our expert team utilizes state-of-the-art technology to conduct thorough assessments, enabling precise diagnoses and personalized treatment plans. We prioritize patient care and closely monitor progress to ensure optimal outcomes and support overall neurological health.

Eye Care

At our lab, we specialize in comprehensive eye care, offering advanced diagnostic services and treatments for a range of conditions, including cataracts, glaucoma, and retinal disorders. Our experienced team utilizes state-of-the-art technology to conduct thorough eye examinations and develop personalized treatment plans. We are committed to ensuring optimal vision health and providing ongoing support to enhance our patients' quality of life.

Hepatology

At our lab, we focus on advanced hepatology services, providing comprehensive evaluations and treatments for liver conditions such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and fatty liver disease. Our expert team employs state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to assess liver health and develop personalized treatment plans. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality care and ongoing support to help our patients achieve optimal liver function and overall well-being.

Pediatrics

At our lab, we specialize in pediatric care, offering comprehensive evaluations and treatments for a wide range of childhood illnesses and developmental concerns. Our experienced team utilizes advanced diagnostic tools to assess the health and well-being of our young patients, ensuring personalized care tailored to their unique needs. We are committed to fostering a supportive environment for children and their families, promoting healthy growth and development at every stage.

Pharmacy

At our lab, we provide comprehensive pharmacy services, focusing on safe and effective medication management for our patients. Our skilled pharmacists utilize advanced technology to ensure accurate dispensing and offer personalized consultations to address individual health needs. We are dedicated to promoting optimal therapeutic outcomes and enhancing patient education, ensuring that each person receives the support necessary for their medication regimen.

Frequently Asked Questions

1.What types of tests and services does your lab offer, and how can they help with my health concerns?
We offer a wide range of tests, including blood work, imaging, and specialized diagnostics, to help identify and manage various health conditions.
2.How long does it typically take to receive test results, and how will I be notified?
Most results are available within 24 to 48 hours, and you will be notified via phone or secure online portal.
3.What measures do you have in place to ensure the accuracy and reliability of your test results?
We follow strict quality control protocols and use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure accurate and reliable results.
4.Do you accept my insurance, and what are the payment options for lab services?
We accept a variety of insurance plans; please contact us to confirm your specific plan. We also offer flexible payment options for self-pay patients.
5.What is the process for scheduling an appointment, and what should I expect when I arrive at the lab?
You can schedule an appointment online or by phone. Upon arrival, you’ll check in, and our staff will guide you through the testing process.
